Why donate blood?

 
Blood is needed every two seconds.Be a Hero. Give Blood.
 
About one in seven people entering a hospital needs blood
 
Only 37 percent of our country's population is eligible to give blood
 
less than 10 percent of those who can donate actually do donate annually.
 
Our blood supply comes from caring donors like you
 
it gives someone another smile, another hug, another chance
